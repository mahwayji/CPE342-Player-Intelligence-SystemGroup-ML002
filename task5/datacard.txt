================================================================================
GAME TERMINOLOGY DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

[1] IP - Internet Protocol address; unique identifier for devices connected to the internet

[2] Game Mode - Different types of gameplay rules or objectives within the same game

[3] ELO - Rating system used to calculate relative skill levels in competitive games

[4] KDA - Kill/Death/Assist ratio; a metric measuring combat performance

[5] In-game Currency - Virtual money used within the game to buy items

[6] Guild/Clan - A group or team of players who play together regularly

[7] VPN - Virtual Private Network; technology that masks a user's real location/IP address
